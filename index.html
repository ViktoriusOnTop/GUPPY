Questions & Answers (Q&A)

Q: How does Guppy AI work?
A: [Our AI uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze medical data and detect patterns indicative of cancer. It is designed to assist healthcare professionals in making informed decisions. Many professionals can still make mistakes, and by our AI we can reduce these mistakes and probably try and help save lives. Even if our AI is not 100% accurate, it can still be a helpful tool for doctors to use as a second opinion. Our AI is not meant to replace doctors, and please inform us if you are a insitute that wants to use this AI to help them research, so that we are able to help you and inform you how t use the AI and how it can assist you in your research.]

There is no required payment, because this is not a business, this is just a project that we wanted to make to help others and make a positive impact in the world. We are not looking to make money, of course, since we are kids and I can't tell the difference between investing and stocks. Completely free to use! Its just that please inform or contact us if you are a insitute or any type of organization that is using our AI to detect melanoma cancer. We do not want to risk the lives of others, and we can help adapt the AI to your liking if you want to use it.]
